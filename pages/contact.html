Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a vehicle?

Simply search for available vehicles, select your dates, and submit a booking request. The merchant will review and approve within 30 minutes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept GCash payments. Payment is processed after merchant approval.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel pending bookings anytime. Approved bookings may have cancellation fees depending on the merchant's policy.

How do I become a merchant?

Visit the "For Merchants" section and complete the registration form. We'll verify your documents within 2-3 business days.
