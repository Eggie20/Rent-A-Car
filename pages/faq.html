Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I create an account?

Click the "Login" button in the navigation and select "Sign up". Fill in your details and verify your email. You're ready to book!

How do I search for vehicles?

Use the search widget on the home page. Select your location, pick-up and return dates, and vehicle type. Click "Search Available Cars" to see results.

How long does merchant approval take?

Most merchants respond within 30 minutes. You'll receive a notification via email and SMS when your request is approved or rejected.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept GCash payments. Payment is processed securely after merchant approval of your booking request.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and partner with trusted payment providers to ensure your information is secure.

Can I modify my booking after submission?

You can modify pending bookings by contacting the merchant directly. Once approved, modifications may incur additional fees.

What is your cancellation policy?

Pending bookings can be cancelled anytime. Approved bookings have a 24-hour cancellation window with full refund. Late cancellations may have fees.

What documents do I need to provide?

You'll need a valid ID and driver's license. These are verified during the booking process to ensure safety for both renters and merchants.

How do I register as a merchant?

Click "For Merchants" in the navigation, then "Register". Fill in your business details and upload required documents. We'll verify within 2-3 business days.

What documents do I need to register?

You'll need: Business Permit, DTI/SEC Registration, Owner's Valid ID, and Vehicle OR/CR copies. Insurance documents are also recommended.

How do I add vehicles to my fleet?

Log in to your merchant dashboard and click "Add New Vehicle". Fill in vehicle details, upload photos, set pricing, and publish. It's that simple!

What are the commission fees?

The Middle Man charges a 10% commission on each booking. This covers platform maintenance, payment processing, and customer support.

How do I receive payments?

Payments are transferred to your registered GCash account within 24-48 hours after the rental is completed and confirmed.

How do I leave a review?

After your rental is completed, you'll receive a prompt to rate your experience. Your review helps other renters make informed decisions.

What if I have an issue during my rental?

Contact the merchant immediately. If you need additional support, reach out to our customer service team via email or phone.
