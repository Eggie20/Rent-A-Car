1. DEPOSIT

A deposit of ₱13,000 to ₱39,000 is required at the time of vehicle collection. The deposit can be paid via GCash, Maya, bank transfer, or card payment. The amount of the deposit depends on the car model and rental agreement. The deposit will be refunded within 14 to 21 business days, provided there are no traffic violations, damages, or additional charges on the vehicle.

2. FUEL

The fuel level of the car must be returned the same as when it was collected. If the fuel level is lower, a charge of ₱260 – ₱455 per missing bar will apply.

3. DRIVER'S LICENSE

For Car Rental in the Philippines

A valid Philippine driver's license is sufficient.

ASEAN Countries

Drivers from ASEAN countries may use their national driving license.

ASEAN member countries are: Brunei, Cambodia, Indonesia, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, the Philippines, Singapore, Thailand, and Vietnam.

  • If your license is in English or Filipino, no extra document is needed.
  • If your license is in another language, you must provide a certified English or Filipino translation.

Non-ASEAN Countries

An International Driving Permit (IDP) is required.

Without an IDP, the rental cannot be processed.

Please prepare the correct documents before pickup. Otherwise, we will be unable to release the vehicle.

4. MILEAGE

Free mileage is 200 km per day. For any mileage exceeding 200 km, an additional charge of ₱13 per km will apply.

5. BUFFER TIME

A one-hour buffer is allowed for both picking up and returning the car. Any delay beyond one hour will incur an additional charge of ₱650 per hour / capped by per day rental rate.

6. CLEANING FEE

If the car is returned excessively dirty (e.g., mud, trash, or stains), a cleaning fee will be charged. The fee amount will depend on the level of cleaning required, starting from ₱195.

7. CANCELLATION POLICY

Free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before the scheduled pick-up time. If you choose to cancel your booking, please note that any bank or transaction fees already charged (e.g., payment gateway processing fees) are non-refundable. The refundable amount will be the balance after deducting such fees, and will be processed within 14 to 21 business days.

Cancellations made within 48 hours of the scheduled pick-up time will incur the full rental charge, and no refund will be issued.

8. RENTAL VEHICLE INSURANCE POLICY

Standard Insurance

Standard insurance covers damage to the rental vehicle, subject to a deductible/excess of ₱104,000 – ₱1,170,000 depending on the car model. It does not provide coverage for third-party liability.

Additional Protection Coverage (APC)

APC is an optional add-on that provides extended protection. It covers damages to the rental vehicle caused by accidents or collisions, including tires, windshield, undercarriage, and glass (if caused by an accident). It reduces your deductible to ₱0 within the following cover range:

  • ₱32,500 maximum coverage (for ₱13,000 deposit)
  • ₱45,500 maximum coverage (for ₱19,500 deposit)
  • ₱65,000 maximum coverage (for ₱26,000 deposit)

Any damages beyond the above limits will be the renter's full responsibility if the rental company's insurance provider cannot approve the claim due to certain special circumstances.

❌ Exclusions:

  • Driving under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or other substances.
  • Unauthorized drivers not listed in the rental agreement.
  • Negligence, such as leaving the car unlocked or keys inside.
  • Off-road use or driving in restricted areas.
  • No coverage on third-party liability.
